To add your own picture:
1. Put your photo in this same folder.
2. Name it profile.jpg.
3. In index.html, change:
   <img src="profile-placeholder.svg" alt="Ryan Jay Villastique profile photo" />
   to:
   <img src="profile.jpg" alt="Ryan Jay Villastique profile photo" />

Recommended photo size: square or portrait, at least 600px wide.
